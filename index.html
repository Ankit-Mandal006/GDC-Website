About Us

Who Are We?

We the Game Developers Community SRM, aim to empower the existing and to nurture the budding game designers and developers in our University. We, as a collective plan to dive headfirst into the ever-expanding world of game development and create experiences worth experiencing.

Mission

Our mission is to foster creativity, collaboration, and technical skills in game development among students by providing a platform for learning and innovation. We aim to empower future game developers through engaging events, practical experiences, and industry exposure, promoting a vibrant gaming culture in our college.

Vision

To be a leading college club that inspires and nurtures the next generation of game developers, creating an ecosystem where students can explore their passion for gaming, develop industry-relevant skills, and establish meaningful connections with professionals in the game development community.
